Four Teachable Concepts · Powered by CrewAI

A critical-thinking rubric students can name.

🎣
Clickbait & Headlines

Does the headline match the story? An XLM-RoBERTa model compares the two.

"Ask: what did the title promise vs. deliver?"
🎭
Emotional Manipulation

A GoEmotions ModernBERT model surfaces anger- or fear-driven language.

"Ask: is this informing me or provoking me?"
⚖️
Bias & Framing

A ModernBERT model (trained on MBIC) flags a Left / Center / Right lean.

"Ask: whose perspective is missing here?"
🕵️
Conspiracy Traits

A 7-trait framework (Vaccine / GMO / 5G) becomes a checklist for reasoning.

"Ask: which of the 7 traits appear?"

Each signal is a real, fine-tuned model — and a concept students can practise, debate, and transfer to any source.
