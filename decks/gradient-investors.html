Product · How It Works

From a live tab to a verdict in four steps

1
Capture
Chrome MV3 extension grabs the active tab's HTML
2
Parse
FastAPI extracts headline + body with Newspaper3k
3
Analyze
CrewAI runs 4 specialist agents, orchestrated by Gemini 2.5 Flash
4
Stream
Results flow back live via Server-Sent Events
HTML payload→ /analyze-html/→ per-request isolated crew→ text/event-stream→ popup UI
